1. EXPERIMENT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This experiment implements and validates a bionic corner detection method based 
on cooperative processing of simple cells and end-stopped cells, simulating the 
hierarchical processing mechanism of the visual cortex.

Key Components:
  - Stage 1: Simple Cell Edge Filtering (Gabor Filter Bank)
  - Stage 2: End-Stopped Cell Dynamic Construction (LoG Operator)
  - Stage 3: Corner Localization (Hierarchical Clustering)
